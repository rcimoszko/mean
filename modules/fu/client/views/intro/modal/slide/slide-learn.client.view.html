Here's some basic terminology to get you started

Moneyline - is simply a bet on a team to win a given game, regardless of the winning margin or points scored.

Spread - a bet where you select for a team to either win by more than a set number of points, or for a team to not lose by more than that set number of points.

Totals (Over/Under) - a bet on the total amount of points scored by both teams in a game.
