Clear Gifs

FansUnite.com’s third party advertisers may use a software on the Website called clear gifs (also known as 1x1 Gifs, Web Beacons, Web Bugs, Pixels or Pixel Tags), that help us better manage content on the Website by allowing us to see what content is relevant to our visitors. Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of Website users. While cookies are stored on a user’s computer hard drive, clear gifs are embedded on Website pages. Gifs are usually about the size of a period. Information gathered by clear gifs cannot be tied to Website visitors’ personally identifiable information.
