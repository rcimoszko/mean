Round Wagers

Over/Under picks are graded on the total number of rounds completed. The official halfway point is 1 minute and 30 seconds into a 3 minute round. 5 minute rounds halway point will be considered 2 minutes and 30 seconds into a round.

Test Matches

In the event a game does not go at least 4 innings, all over/under wagers will be returned.

Tied test matches will result in units wagered being returned.

Highest and Lowest Team Totals

If teams tie for the highest team total, the winner will be the team with the largest winning margin. If the winning margins are equal, all picks on the tied teams will be graded as winners.

If teams tie for the lowest team total, the winner will be the team with the largest losing margin. If the losing margins are equal, all picks on the tied teams will be graded as winners.

Matchups

Both golfers must tee off in order for wagers to be graded.

Player with the lowest score over the entire tournament is the matchup winner.

If one player misses the cut, the other play automatically wins. If both players miss the cut, the lower score of the two will be deemed the winner.

Tournament Winner

Golf Three-Way (Three Balls)

Picks on "Three Balls", will be decided by the lowest score after one full round of play (18 holes).

Once a player has started, if they withdraw before completing 18 holes they are deemed the loser, regardless of the score.
