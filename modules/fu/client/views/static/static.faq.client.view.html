RANKING

Profit is calculated as your return, less the units wagered. Return on investment is calculated as the return, divided by the amount wagered. Average odds are the total summation of odds that you have wagered, divided by the number of picks you have placed. Average bet size is the total summation of units that you have wagered, divided by the number of picks you have placed.
